Rural Health Care Notice

You may be entitled to coverage for health care services from the following non- participating providers if you live or reside within 30 paved road miles of the listed providers, or if you live or reside in closer proximity to the listed providers than to Arches participating providers:

  • Green Valley/River Clinic, Green River, Emery/Grand Counties, Utah
  • Halchita Clinic, San Juan County, Utah
  • Monument Valley Health Center,Monument Valley, Utah

This list may change periodically. Please check our website or call for veriﬁcation. Please be advised that if you choose a non-participating provider you will be responsible for any charges not covered by your Arches beneﬁt plan.

If you have questions concerning your rights to see a provider on this list, you may contact Customer Service at (801) 323-6200 or 1-800-377-4161. If we do not resolve your problem, you may contact the Oﬃce of Consumer Health Assistance in the Utah Insurance Department at 1-866-350-6242.
